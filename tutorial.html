Tutorial

You may have already checked out the rules before, but just in case you wanted to refer them again here, they are attached below.

  • Press any key to start the game.
  • Make a mental note of the colour that flashes on screen.
  • Before clicking on current colour, remember the sequence of colours previously flashed(if any)
  • Click in that particular order.
  • Messing up at any moment in the sequence will end the game!
